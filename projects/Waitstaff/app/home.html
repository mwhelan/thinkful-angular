New Meal

Select 'New Meal' from the menu. Enter in the following information on the Enter the Meal Details panel and click the submit button

  • Base Meal Price ($): The meal price, in dollars.
  • Tax Rate (%): The percentage of sales tax. A number between 1 and 100.
  • Tip Rate (%): The percentage of the tip. A number between 1 and 100.

When you click the submit button the following information is calculated in the Customer Charges panel.

  • Subtotal: The cost of the meal plus tax.
  • Tip: The dollar value of the tip.
  • Total: The total dollar value of the subtotal plus the tip.

This information is also added to your total earnings, which you can see if you select the "My Earnings Info" option from the menu

My Earnings

Select 'My Earnings' from the menu. This panel displays cumulative information about all of the meals submitted so far.

  • Tip Total: The sum of all tips calculated so far.
  • Meal count: The total number of meals entered into the calculator.
  • Average Tip Per Meal: The total tip value divided by the number of meals.
